# EU/NL Legal Source Standard

## Acceptable primary sources
- EUR-Lex
- EDPB
- Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens
- ACM
- ConsuWijzer
- Business.gov.nl
- Rijksoverheid
- Belastingdienst
- Official Dutch legislation portals
- Official court decisions where directly relevant

## Required legal conclusion format

### Issue
The exact legal or regulatory question.

### Verified source
Official source, issuing body, date, and access date.

### Verified rule
A careful paraphrase of the applicable rule.

### Application to VANTAM
Why the rule is potentially relevant to the actual business model and website.

### Limitation
Missing facts, scope uncertainty, exceptions, or account-level uncertainty.

### Classification
One of:
- binding requirement;
- official guidance;
- owner decision;
- recommendation;
- unresolved;
- professional-review item;
- launch blocker.

### Action
What must be confirmed, changed, documented, or professionally reviewed.
