# VANTAM Invoice Wording Guide

**Internal guide only. This is not an invoice generator and does not set VAT rates for specific services.**

## 1. Identity Details to Include

Legal / trade name: VANTAM Services
Public brand, if used: VANTAM
Legal form: Dutch eenmanszaak
KvK number: 42080058
Business address: BUSINESS ADDRESS REQUIRED BEFORE SENDING
BTW ID: [EXACT COMPLETE BTW ID FROM BELASTINGDIENST DOCUMENT]
HARD STOP — do not send or accept this document until the real business address has replaced the placeholder.

Never include the private omzetbelastingnummer, BSN, or any invented address or VAT ID.

## 2. Invoice Core Fields

Each invoice should include:

- invoice number;
- invoice date;
- service date or service period;
- client identity and client address;
- clear service description;
- net price;
- applicable VAT rate, confirmed for the specific service;
- VAT amount;
- total amount including VAT;
- currency;
- payment term, such as due within 3 calendar days where agreed;
- payment details placeholder;
- reference to the accepted Offer / appendix where useful.

## 3. VAT Presentation

VANTAM is registered for VAT and does not use KOR. The applicable VAT rate must be confirmed for the specific service before invoicing. Do not invent VAT rates for service categories.

Where an invoice draft legally requires the trader-information block, use the exact BTW ID line shown above.

## 4. Consultation Credit

Where consultation credit applies, show it as a separate line, for example:

"Consultation credit applied once against eligible package accepted within 14 calendar days: EUR [AMOUNT]."

The credit is not exchangeable for cash, cannot exceed the package price, and cannot be used more than once.

## 5. Approved Third-Party Costs

Approved third-party costs should be listed separately from VANTAM service fees. Include the provider / recipient, description, amount, and VAT treatment if known.

Do not invoice unapproved third-party costs unless mandatory law or the accepted agreement clearly permits it.

## 6. Corrections and Credit Notes

If an invoice needs correction, do not silently overwrite the issued invoice. Issue a corrected invoice or credit note according to accounting requirements and keep the audit trail.

Correction / credit-note wording should identify:

- original invoice number;
- correction reason;
- corrected amount;
- VAT correction, if any;
- date of correction.

## 7. Consumer Late-Payment Reminder

For consumer clients, late-payment enforcement must respect mandatory Dutch reminder, interest, and collection-cost rules. Send any required statutory reminder before charging collection costs. Do not state automatic collection costs, arbitrary penalties, or unsupported statutory percentages in the invoice.

## 8. Prohibited Invoice Wording

Do not include:

- private omzetbelastingnummer;
- BSN;
- invented VAT ID;
- invented business address;
- hidden administration fees;
- open-ended expense lines;
- automatic non-refundable wording;
- housing success fee;
- tenant-side brokerage or mediation remuneration pending specialist Dutch review.
