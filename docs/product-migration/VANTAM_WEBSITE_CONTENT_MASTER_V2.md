# VANTAM Website Content Master V2

## Core Message
Student-led practical support for international students in the Netherlands.

## Public Sections
- Hero: practical support before, during, and after arrival.
- Entry logic: choose a path based on what the visitor needs now.
- Process: Assess, Map, Act & coordinate, Verify & continue.
- Services: the main ladder and the separate housing activation path.
- Contact: one form with context-aware prefills.

## English Copy Rules
- Use concise, direct phrases.
- Keep package names exactly as approved.
- Keep housing as a separate activation path.
- Avoid any wording that implies a guaranteed result, a dashboard, a portal, or a public SLA.

## Ukrainian Copy Rules
- Preserve the same commercial meaning as English.
- Do not introduce extra promises or broader scope than English.

## Russian Copy Rules
- Preserve the same commercial meaning as English.
- Do not introduce extra promises or broader scope than English.
